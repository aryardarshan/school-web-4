Brigade school

The Brigade Schools have earned the reputation of being institutions of all round excellence. Our three schools at JP Nagar, Mahadevapura and Malleswaram, give the children a head start with the benefits of a unique, Montessori based pre-primary education. Our exceptional curriculum, along with co-curricular activities is designed with extra care. Our schools have been instituted by Brigade Foundation as part of its interest in Education, Health and Community Service. Our strong teaching staff, with supportive parents and enthusiastic students contribute to the success of the school. Our students have developed into confident young adults with a strong sense of awareness. In our three schools we recognize that all students are uniquely talented. The schools provide opportunities for children to do whatever they aspire to do - they could be future leaders of the country, get into premier institutions and follow their dreams! The facilities and excellent team of facilitators help the students develop positive relationships and the opportunity to realize their potential to the fullest. We provide an exceptionally caring and learning-focused environment for our students.

Admissions

Thank you for your interest in applying to The Brigade Schools. The admission process at The Brigade Schools is a very simple one. For In-depth understanding of the admission procedure, please read the respective instructions very carefully. REGISTRATIONS ARE OPEN FOR THE ACADEMIC SESSION 2021-22 FROM MONDAY 21ST SEPTEMBER 2020. Seats are available in: JP Nagar School—Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class 3 to 9 & Class 11. Limited seats in Class 1 and 2 Mahadevapura School—Nursery to Class 8 Malleswaram School—Nursery, Class 7 to 9 The Application Process to The Brigade School begins with submitting a fully filled Expression of interest form. The Expression of Interest form can either be submitted On-line or you can download the Off-line form, print it, fill it and submit it at the school.
